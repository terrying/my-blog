- **影响资产**: GitLab 平台
- **预估影响**: 数千个
- **EPSS**: 0.13848 (percentile 0.94274) @ 2026-04-06

**描述**: ruby-saml provides security assertion markup language (SAML) single sign-on (SSO) for Ruby.
