- **严重程度**: 🔴 CRITICAL
- **风险等级**: 极高风险 (5/5)
- **影响资产**: 未知
- **预估影响**: 数千个
- **EPSS**: 0.02803 (percentile 0.84701) @ 2026-06-30

**描述**: The Keras Model.load_model function permits arbitrary code execution, even with safe_mode&#x3D;True, through a manually constructed, malicious .keras archive.
